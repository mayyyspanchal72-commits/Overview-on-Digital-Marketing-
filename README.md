# Digital Marketing Industry & Agency Analysis Report

This repository contains a comprehensive academic project report detailing the operations, strategies, and market landscape of the digital marketing industry. The report was prepared for Sharnbasva University, Kalaburagi.

The core of the project is a detailed case study of **Click Link**, a digital marketing agency based in Bidar, Karnataka.

## Project Overview

This report provides a multi-faceted analysis of digital marketing, combining theoretical frameworks with practical agency analysis and primary market research.

The project is divided into two main parts:
* **Part A:** A comprehensive analysis of the digital marketing industry and a deep dive into the Click Link agency, covering its profile, services, strategies, and structure.
* **Part B:** A primary research study, including a survey and data analysis, to understand market perceptions and behaviors.

---

## Key Sections of the Report

###  Part A: Industry & Company Analysis (Click Link)

This section provides a complete business analysis of the Click Link agency.

* **Industry Profile:** Introduction to the digital marketing domain.
* **Company Profile:** In-depth details of Click Link, including its founding (2022), location (Bidar), services, and mission.
* **Business Frameworks:** Application of key business models to analyze the agency:
    * **McKinsey 7S Framework:** Analyzing Click Link's Strategy, Structure, Systems, Shared Values, Skills, Style, and Staff.
    * **SWOT Analysis:** A detailed breakdown of the agency's Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats.
* **Operational Profile:** An overview of the company's service profile, workflow model, infrastructure, and future growth prospects.

###  Part B: Data Analysis & Research

This section outlines the research methodology and presents the findings from a primary survey.

* **Research Methodology:** The study uses a Descriptive Research Design with a convenience sampling technique.
* **Statement of the Problem:** Addresses the challenges businesses face in the evolving digital landscape.
* **Data Analysis & Interpretation:**
    * **Note:** The survey data presented in this section was collected in reference to **Empower Creative Consulting LLP**.
    * **Key Survey Findings:** Includes respondent data on:
        * Primary sources of product information (e.g., Advertisements, Friends).
        * Most important media forms (e.g., Online, TV).
        * Experience with online purchasing and SEO tool awareness.

---

##  Findings & Suggestions

The analysis concluded with several key findings and strategic suggestions, including:

* **Findings:** The agency demonstrates high client satisfaction but faces challenges in broad brand awareness within a competitive market.
* **Suggestions:** Key recommendations include refining the value proposition, enhancing marketing communications to increase brand awareness, and further leveraging data analytics.
